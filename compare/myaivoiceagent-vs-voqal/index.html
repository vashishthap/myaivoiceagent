MyAIVoiceAgent vs Voqal AI: which UK AI receptionist is right for you?

Two of the more credible UK-focused AI receptionist services in May 2026. They overlap on the obvious things — British voice, 24/7 answering, appointment booking — and diverge on price, configuration model, and vertical depth. Here's an honest read on which one fits which kind of business, including where Voqal genuinely beats us.

Quick verdict

If you run a single-site practice, salon, or restaurant, you're comfortable spending two evenings configuring an agent yourself, and you want the lowest possible monthly cost from day one — Voqal AI is the better choice. £197/month, no setup fee, live in hours.

If you run a UK dental practice (especially anything NHS-touching), a multi-site group, a salon chain, or any business where you want the agent built, validated, and maintained for you in five days — MyAIVoiceAgent is the better choice. £200–£500/month after a £500–£1,000 one-time setup, including done-for-you configuration and ongoing prompt maintenance.

The honest summary: Voqal wins on price and time-to-live for confident DIYers. We win on vertical depth (NHS dental, multi-language restaurants, emergency-routing trades) and done-for-you handholding. Both produce a credibly British call experience — neither will embarrass you.

Where Voqal is better

Honest about where the competition wins. Three real advantages, and one nuance.

1. Lower entry price and zero setup fee

At £197/month with no setup fee, Voqal is the cheapest credible UK-focused AI receptionist on day one. For a single-site business that wants to test the category for three months before committing, that lower entry cost is genuinely meaningful. Our £500–£1,000 setup fee is a real barrier for cautious buyers, and we're honest about it. If your call volume is modest and you don't need bespoke vertical flows, Voqal's pricing is the rational choice.

2. Faster time-to-live for confident DIYers

Voqal's self-serve dashboard genuinely lets you go from sign-up to a live agent answering your number in a few hours, assuming you know your call flows and your calendar is plugged in. Our 5-day done-for-you process feels slow next to that — and for some businesses, slow is the wrong trade. If you're a tech-confident operator who already has clear call scripts and a Google Calendar that works, Voqal removes the middleman.

3. Cleaner self-serve product surface

Voqal's dashboard is well-designed for users who want to tweak prompts, swap voices, and read transcripts themselves. Our portal is more limited because most of our customers don't want to touch the underlying prompts — they want us to do it. If self-service is a feature for you, not a chore, Voqal's product is genuinely nicer to live in week-on-week.

The nuance: voice quality is a tie

Both products use ElevenLabs voices in May 2026 and both sound credibly British. We do per-industry prosody calibration; Voqal ships a sensible default. In blind A/B tests with UK callers, the gap is within margin of error. If anyone tells you their AI receptionist sounds "obviously more human" than the other in 2026, ask for the audio.

Where we're better

Five places we believe MyAIVoiceAgent earns its setup fee.

1. NHS dental, out of the box

A UK dental practice taking NHS patients needs a receptionist who understands UDA bands, recall logic, NHS 111 handover, and urgent dental care criteria. Our dental call flow ships with all of this pre-built and validated against real UK practice management systems — Dentally, EXACT, and Software of Excellence. Voqal can be configured for dental, but you write the NHS triage logic yourself, and getting it wrong is a clinical-risk issue, not a UX one. If you're an NHS-mixed practice, this is the single biggest reason buyers choose us. See our dental lander for the full flow.

2. Multi-vertical depth that actually pronounces things correctly

Restaurants taking takeaway orders need an agent that pronounces "biryani", "dosa", "ossobuco", and "linguine" cleanly to a UK caller. Salons need stylist-level booking ("a half-head highlights with Yasmin, not Sarah"). Trades need emergency-versus-quote routing. Each of these is a multi-week tuning job we've already done. Voqal gives you a clean canvas; we give you a finished oil painting in your industry.

3. Built-in multi-site routing

A 3-site dental group routing patients across Bristol, Bath, and Cheltenham needs more than a single agent. Our Premier plan handles site-aware routing (which calendar to book into, which clinician's holiday calendar to skip, which site's emergency triage to follow). Voqal can do it if you wire it up; we do it for you. See the multi-practice case study.

4. Done-for-you ongoing prompt maintenance

AI agents drift. New treatments launch. A receptionist quits and your call volume changes. A new product provider gets onboarded. Every Pro and Premier plan with us includes ongoing prompt edits as part of the monthly fee — you email us, we update the agent, you don't think about it. Voqal expects you to maintain prompts yourself. That works for some teams; it's a hidden cost for others.

5. UK-built, UK-staffed, UK-supported

Both products are credible in the UK, but our team is UK-based and our support hours are UK business hours. When something goes sideways at 9.30am on a Monday, the person fixing it is in the same time zone. Voqal is also UK-friendly, but the home of self-serve support is a chat widget rather than a dedicated success manager.

Frequently asked questions

Is Voqal AI cheaper than MyAIVoiceAgent?

Yes on the entry tier and on day one. Voqal is £197/mo with no setup fee; we're £200/mo with a £500–£1,000 setup. Over 12 months a single-site customer pays roughly £500–£1,000 less with Voqal for a comparable simple setup. The trade-off is configuration time and vertical depth.

Does Voqal AI work for UK dental practices?

It can be configured for dental, but it doesn't ship with NHS-band logic, UDA terminology, or NHS 111 triage built in. We do. For an NHS-mixed practice this is the single most consequential difference.

Can I switch from Voqal to MyAIVoiceAgent?

Yes. Most switches happen when a practice outgrows self-serve. We port the Twilio number, rebuild call flows, and run a soft-launch week. 5–10 working days end-to-end.

Which has the better British voice?

Both use ElevenLabs and both sound credibly British. We calibrate per industry; Voqal ships a sensible default. In blind tests the gap is within margin of error.

Do either host data in the UK or EU?

Both can route through EU/UK regions. Voqal documents EU residency; we store recordings in eu-west-1 and sign a UK GDPR DPA on every plan. Confirm specifics with each vendor before signing — third-party regions change.
