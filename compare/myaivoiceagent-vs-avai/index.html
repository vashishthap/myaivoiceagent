MyAIVoiceAgent vs AVAI: which UK AI receptionist is right for you?

AVAI (aivoiceautomation.co.uk) and MyAIVoiceAgent are two UK-built AI voice services that often appear on the same shortlist. They look similar from the outside — British voice, multi-vertical, done-for-you — and diverge sharply on pricing transparency, outbound capability, and how vertical depth is delivered. Here's an honest read on which one fits which kind of business, including the things AVAI does better than us.

Quick verdict

If outbound voice campaigns are core to your use case — lead qualification, dental recall, reactivation, sales prospecting — AVAI is the better choice. Their outbound stack is genuinely more mature than ours, and we don't try to compete on it.

If you want predictable, published flat-fee pricing, a UK dental practice taking NHS work, a multi-vertical inbound use case (dental + salon + restaurant + trades), or you simply want to know your exact monthly bill before a sales call — MyAIVoiceAgent is the better choice. Three published tiers (£200/£350/£500), a one-time setup, no negotiation.

The honest summary: AVAI wins on outbound and on bespoke enterprise configuration. We win on pricing transparency, NHS dental depth, and predictable inbound-only operations. Both sound credibly British on a real call. The right pick depends on whether you need outbound voice and whether published pricing matters to your buying process.

Where AVAI is better

Three places AVAI genuinely beats us. We won't pretend otherwise.

1. Outbound voice campaigns

AVAI runs autonomous outbound voice campaigns at meaningful volume — lead-qualification waves, dental recall campaigns, reactivation calls to lapsed customers, and B2B prospecting. Their outbound stack is more mature than anything we ship, and we made the deliberate choice in 2026 not to build outbound voice. We use SMS and WhatsApp for review-chasing and confirmation, which works for most SMB inbound use cases — but if you want an AI that calls out to 500 lapsed patients next Tuesday, that's not us. AVAI does that, and does it well. If outbound is more than 20% of what you want from this category, stop reading and go talk to AVAI.

2. Bespoke enterprise configuration

AVAI's not-publicly-priced model is genuinely better suited to enterprise procurement processes that expect a bespoke statement of work. If you're an established UK group buying for 10+ sites and your procurement team wants a custom contract, AVAI's quote-based engagement is more natural than fitting into our published Premier tier. We can flex Premier for groups, but AVAI's whole model is built around bespoke. For a 50-site dental group or a multi-region salon chain with non-standard requirements, that bespoke model is a real fit.

3. Established multi-year operating track record

AVAI has been operating in the UK voice automation space longer than us, and that maturity shows in some operational details — particularly outbound campaign management and integration with enterprise telephony stacks (Microsoft Teams, contact centre platforms). If your buying committee weighs vendor longevity heavily, AVAI's track record is a real factor. We're transparent about being newer; we don't pretend otherwise on a sales call.

Where we're better

Five places we believe MyAIVoiceAgent earns the deal.

1. Pricing transparency

We publish three flat tiers on /pricing: £200 Starter, £350 Pro, £500 Premier. Setup is £500 to £1,000, also published. You know your bill before you book a demo. AVAI does not publish entry pricing, and quotes are bespoke. For SMB buyers — especially in regulated sectors where budget pre-approval matters — that opacity is a real friction. We made the decision to publish pricing precisely because most UK SMBs hate the "request a quote" dance, and we trust the market to reward us for it.

2. NHS dental depth, out of the box

Our dental call flow ships with UDA terminology, NHS bands 1/2/3, NHS 111 emergency handover, and urgent dental care criteria pre-built and validated against real UK practice management systems — Dentally, EXACT, and Software of Excellence. AVAI is multi-vertical and can be configured for dental, but does not ship with NHS-specific flows pre-built. For an NHS-mixed UK practice, that pre-built depth is the single biggest reason buyers pick us. See our dental lander.

3. Predictable, fixed-volume monthly cost

Each of our tiers includes a generous published call-volume cap. You know what you're paying every month. AVAI's bespoke pricing typically ties more closely to outbound campaign volume, which is the right model for outbound use cases but introduces variability that makes inbound-only budgeting harder. If you want a single line item on your P&L that doesn't move month to month, our flat fee is what you want.

4. Five-day go-live

We commit to five working days from contract signature to live agent answering your number. This is hard-coded into our setup process: scoping call day 1, call-flow design days 2–3, voice calibration day 4, soft-launch day 5. AVAI's bespoke timeline is more flexible — sometimes faster for simple deployments, sometimes considerably longer for complex enterprise scopes. If speed-to-live is the deciding factor, our published five days is the safer bet.

5. Done-for-you ongoing prompt maintenance, included

Every Pro and Premier plan includes ongoing prompt edits as part of the monthly fee. New treatment launched? New stylist hired? Menu changed for the seasonal special? You email us, we update the agent within one business day, no extra charge. AVAI's bespoke model usually has separate change-request economics. For a fast-moving SMB, the "just email us" model removes a meaningful operational tax.

Frequently asked questions

How much does AVAI cost in the UK?

AVAI does not publish entry pricing as of May 2026. Engagements typically start around £500/mo with a custom setup, and rise with outbound volume. We publish three tiers (£200/£350/£500). Always request a written quote before comparing.

Does AVAI offer outbound calling?

Yes — outbound is one of AVAI's headline differentiators (lead qualification, recall, reactivation). We're intentionally inbound-only and use SMS/WhatsApp for review-chasing. If outbound voice is a real requirement, AVAI is the better fit.

Which is better for UK dental practices?

For NHS-mixed practices, MyAIVoiceAgent — we ship NHS bands, UDA logic, 111 triage, and Dentally/EXACT/SOE integrations pre-built. For private clinics also running outbound recall campaigns, AVAI's outbound capability tilts the decision.

Is AVAI's pricing transparent?

No — AVAI quotes bespoke. We publish three flat tiers. If transparent monthly cost is a primary buying factor, that's a meaningful difference.

Can I switch between AVAI and MyAIVoiceAgent?

Yes. Both use UK Twilio numbers, so porting is straightforward. We run a parallel soft-launch week so callers experience no downtime. 5–10 working days end-to-end.
