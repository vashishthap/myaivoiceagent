How to fix it

Missed calls fall into three buckets. Each has a different fix.

  1. Calls during opening hours that ring out. Usually because your team is on another call or with a customer. The simplest fix is a second line plus an AI agent that picks up overflow on the third ring. Most callers don't realise they've been re-routed.
  2. Calls outside opening hours. The largest bucket for almost every UK SMB. Evenings, weekends, bank holidays, and the half-hour either side of close. AI handles these natively because there's no human on shift to compete with.
  3. Calls that hit voicemail. Voicemail kills conversion. Industry data consistently shows fewer than one in five callers leave a message, and almost none ring back. Replacing voicemail with a live answer captures the lead while intent is hot.

For most UK small businesses, an AI receptionist closes the gap on all three at the same price as a few hours of part-time human cover. If you want to see the maths for a real call flow rather than a sliders model, the cost breakdown article walks through it pound by pound.
