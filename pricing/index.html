Frequently asked questions

How much does an AI voice agent cost in the UK?

From £200/month plus a one-time £500 setup. Three flat tiers — no per-minute surprises, no contracts.

Are there long-term contracts?

No. Month-to-month with 30 days' notice to cancel.

How quickly can we go live?

5 working days from the scoping call. Configuration, voice calibration, and Twilio bridging all happen in that window.

Is the agent GDPR-compliant?

Yes. EU data residency, retention controls, and a DPA available on request.

What happens if call volume exceeds my plan?

We alert you at 80% and recommend an upgrade. Calls are never throttled — overage is charged at a flat per-call fee.
