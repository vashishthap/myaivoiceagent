Frequently asked questions

Can the AI tell a real emergency from a non-urgent job?

Yes — symptom-led triage. Water loss, no heating with vulnerable occupants, sparking, exposed wiring, gas smell. You set the escalation rules; the agent follows them.

What happens to an emergency call at 2am?

The agent captures the symptoms, address, and access details, then calls or SMSes your on-call engineer with a one-line summary. Gas-leak symptoms route the caller to 0800 111 999 immediately.

Will it qualify leads or just take messages?

Qualifies. Job type, postcode, rough scope, ideal date, domestic vs commercial. Out-of-area and wrong-trade calls are politely declined.

How does it book without double-booking us?

Live Google Calendar read/write. The agent only offers free slots and respects travel time between postcodes. Out-of-hours bookings get a confirmation call back.

Will my customers know it's AI?

Natural British voice (Matilda, ElevenLabs). Only discloses if asked directly. Most callers treat the agent as a normal call handler.

Is it Gas Safe or NICEIC compliant?

It's a call-handling layer, not a regulated trade. The agent never gives technical advice and defers all gas/electrical safety questions to the engineer. Your registration number is included in confirmation SMS.
