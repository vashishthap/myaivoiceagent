FAQ

Common questions

Will it sound robotic or artificial?
No. We use ElevenLabs premium voice synthesis to produce natural, conversational British English. Most callers genuinely can't tell the difference. You'll hear a live demo call before we go live.
What if the agent can't answer a question?
The agent is trained to gracefully offer a callback or transfer to a real person if the enquiry falls outside its scope. No caller is ever left confused or abandoned.
Do I need any technical knowledge?
None whatsoever. We handle everything from script to go-live. You approve the agent and take the calls — that's it.
Can I keep my existing phone number?
Yes. We provide a dedicated line you can either promote directly or forward your existing number to. Number porting is also available.
What's the contract length?
Month-to-month, no lock-in. Most clients stay because it works — not because they're contractually obligated to.
